Biology

Course Overview

This course includes a study of living organisms, from the simplest to the most complex.

It tackles the anatomy, development, genetics, and evolution of different species.

The course explains the processes of growth and development in individuals and populations.

It covers biological concepts like the chemical basis of life, cell structure and function,

metabolism, reproduction, biological diversity and classification, plant structure and function, and many more.
