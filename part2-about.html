Mission

The Phillipine Science High School, operating under one System of Governance and Management, provides scholarship to students with high aptitude in science and mathematics.


The PSHS System offers an education that is humanistic in spirit, global in perspective, and patriotic in orientation. It is based on a curriculum that emphasizes science and mathematics and the development of well-rounded individuals.


The PSHS System prepares its student for careers in Science and Technology and contributes to nation building by helping the country attain a critical mass of professionals and leaders in Science and Technology.

Vision

We are the leading science high school in the Asia Pacific Region preparing our scholars to become globally competitive

Filipino scientists equipped with 21st century skills and imbued with the core values of truth excellence, and service to nation
